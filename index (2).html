About Oyster Bay

Oyster Bay is a fine dining restaurant offering delicious meals, quality drinks, and a relaxed urban ambience. A perfect place to unwind after a long week.

Our culinary team combines traditional techniques with modern flair to create unforgettable dishes using the freshest local ingredients. Whether you're joining us for a business lunch, romantic dinner, or weekend brunch, we provide an exceptional dining experience.

With our elegant yet comfortable atmosphere, attentive service, and carefully curated wine list, Oyster Bay has become a favorite destination for food enthusiasts in Nairobi.
